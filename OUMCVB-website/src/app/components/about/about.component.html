About The Oakland Men's Volleyball Club

About Us

We're just a bunch of dudes that love volleyball. We're dedicated to growing the community and raising the bar for men's volleyball. If you love volleyball, come join us!

Practice Times and Tryouts for Fall 2025

Practices will be held every Monday and Wednesday from 5:00 PM to 7:00 PM at the Oakland University Rec Center.

Tryouts for the Fall 2025 season will take place on Wednesday, September 13th and Monday, September 18th, from 5:00 PM to 7:00 PM. All skill levels are welcome!
